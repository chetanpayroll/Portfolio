Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

At Chetan Sharma's Portfolio ("we," "our," or "us"), we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

This website serves as a professional portfolio to showcase payroll implementation experience, expertise, and projects to potential employers, HR leaders, and industry professionals. We are not a business entity selling goods or services directly through this site.

1. Information We Collect

We believe in data minimization and only collect information that is absolutely necessary for the functioning and improvement of this portfolio.

  • Voluntarily Provided Information: We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you use our "Connect" or "Contact" forms, such as your name, email address, phone number, and any message content.
  • Usage Data: Like many websites, we may use standard analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics) to collect non-personal information about your visit. This includes your browser type, device type, time spent on pages, and referring websites. This data helps us understand how our portfolio is being viewed and improve the user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for professional purposes solely related to the intent of this portfolio:

  • To respond to your inquiries, job opportunities, or collaboration requests.
  • To analyze website traffic and optimize the presentation of professional content.
  • To ensure the security and proper technical functioning of the website.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. Your data is treated with the utmost confidentiality suitable for a professional relationship.

We may share generic aggregated demographic information not linked to any personal identification information regarding visitors and users with our partners or for professional analytics purposes.

4. Third-Party Links

Our portfolio may contain links to third-party websites (e.g., LinkedIn, GitHub, project demos). These external sites have separate and independent privacy policies. We therefore have no responsibility or liability for the content and activities of these linked sites. Nonetheless, we seek to protect the integrity of our site and welcome any feedback about these sites.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate data collection, storage, and processing practices and security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We have the discretion to update this privacy policy at any time. When we do, we will revise the updated date at the top of this page. We encourage you to frequently check this page for any changes to stay informed about how we are helping to protect the personal information we collect.
