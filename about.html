My Story

My journey in payroll didn't start with a grand plan to revolutionize the industry, but it began with a simple curiosity about how large organizations manage their most critical asset: their people. Over the last 13 years, that curiosity has evolved into a deep passion for the complex machinery of global compensation.

Starting at Aon Hewitt, I learned the fundamentals of benefits administration and the importance of precision. A single decimal point error can impact an employee's livelihood, a responsibility I've never taken lightly. As I moved to Xerox and then Deel, the scope of my work expanded from local processing to global strategy.

I realized that as companies grow, their payroll systems often fail to keep up. Processes remain manual, data lives in silos, and compliance becomes a ticking time bomb. This is where I found my niche: bridging the gap between chaotic growth and structured efficiency.

Today, I focus on implementation and automation. I don't just "run payroll"; I build the engines that make payroll run itself. Whether it's designing a validation algorithm that catches errors before they happen or migrating 2,000 employees to a new platform without a single missed payment, I thrive on the challenge of making the complex feel simple.
