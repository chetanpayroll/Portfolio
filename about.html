My Story

With over 13 years in global payroll operations, I've had the privilege of working with some of the world's leading organizations to build, migrate, and optimize payroll systems across multiple countries. My journey began at AON Hewitt, where I developed my foundation in benefits administration and HR operations. From there, I moved to Xerex Business Services, expanding into multi-country payroll processing across APAC.

Today, as Global Payroll Implementation Manager at Deel, I lead complex implementation projects spanning 25+ countries, while building automation tools that have transformed how our teams work. I realized that as companies grow, their payroll systems often fail to keep up -- processes remain manual, data lives in silos, and compliance becomes a ticking time bomb.

This is where I found my niche: bridging the gap between chaotic growth and structured efficiency. I don't just "run payroll"; I build the engines that make payroll run itself. Whether it's designing a validation algorithm that catches errors before they happen or migrating 2,000 employees to a new platform without a single missed payment, I thrive on the challenge of making the complex feel simple.
